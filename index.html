This is the previous EPCS logo.

It reminds us that the HOME, the CHURCH, and the SCHOOL all contribute to raising children in the Lord.

The BIBLE and the CROSS symbolize the truth of Scripture and the gospel of Jesus.

The new school symbols are built around those same three words: HOME, CHURCH, and SCHOOL.

The word HOME is at the base of the triangle because parents have primary responsibility to raise children in the Lord.

"Fathers... bring them up
in the discipline and instruction of the Lord"
(Eph. 6:4, ESV)
